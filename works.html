Gameography

Zom Bam Bros

March 15, 2018

The third and final game I helped make for my Game Engine Lab, made in collaboration with Ittai Mann, Andrew Smith, and John Luke Saake. Players are placed in a top-down, zombie-infested city and are forced to fight off the hordes, and eventually, each other. I wrote 2 songs, created all the sound effects, and assisted in level design.

Punch Kick Super

February 28, 2018

Collaboration with Juwin Viray for my Game Engine Lab. A combination of Rock, Paper, Scissors and the classic Quick Draw games themed around fighting games. I wrote the main theme, ambient city noise, all sound effects, and implemented nested coroutines as random countdown timers.

Reef

January 25, 2018

A simple 2D ocean exploration game, made in about one week for my first piece of Game Engine Lab coursework. My very first foray into solo Unity/C# development. You can play it by clicking its name above. Please note that the game wasn't built for the web, so try downloading it instead: Win | Mac
