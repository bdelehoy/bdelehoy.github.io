Gameography

Buddy
September 2, 2018

A game made in collaboration with Kyle Kissler for the Game Maker's Toolkit Game Jam 2018.
The concept is simple: a stealth game where the objective is to attract as much attention to yourself as possible. You can download it by clicking on Buddy above.

Zom Bam Bros

March 15, 2018

The third and final game I helped make for my Game Engine Lab, made in collaboration with Ittai Mann, Andrew Smith, and John Luke Saake. Players are placed in a top-down, zombie-infested city and are forced to fight off the hordes, and eventually, each other. I wrote 2 songs, created all the sound effects, and assisted with level design.

Punch Kick Super

February 28, 2018

Collaboration with Juwin Viray for my Game Engine Lab. A combination of Rock, Paper, Scissors and the classic Quick Draw games themed around fighting games. I wrote the main theme, created all sound effects, and implemented nested coroutines as random countdown timers.

Reef
January 25, 2018

A simple 2D ocean exploration game, made in about one week for my first piece of Game Engine Lab coursework. My very first foray into solo Unity/C# development. You can play it by clicking its name above. Some parts might look strange in-browser, so I recommend downloading it instead:

Forest Runner

October 27-29, 2017

An aptly titled infinite runner made for the UCI Video Game Development Club's Fall 2017 "Proto" Game Jam with team members Justin Lee, Jason Mehroziad, Timothy Quach, and Brenda Rendon. I helped design the game and contributed all the audio. You can download it on my itch.io page!
