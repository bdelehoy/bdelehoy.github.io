Music & Sound Design Reel

Here you can find samples of composition and sound design work I've done for the Video Game Development Club (VGDC) at UC Irvine - and beyond!

All the audio on this page was made using Image-Line's FL Studio and plug-ins such as Serum, Harmor, and Kontakt. Some sound effects are synthesized from scratch, others feature layering of multiple samples, but most use both techniques.
